Logging In

1

Open Sign In

Tap the Sign In button at the top-right of the homepage.

2

Enter Your Credentials

Type your Email Address and Password. Tap the eye icon if you need to double-check your password.

3

Sign In or Use Google

Click Sign In — or tap Google Sign-In for instant access without typing a password.

4

Two-Factor Authentication

If you enabled 2FA, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy).

5

Forgot Password?

Tap Forgot password? to receive a reset link via email. Follow the link to set a new password.

lightbulb Pro Tip:

Enable 2FA in Security Settings for extra account protection — it only takes 30 seconds to set up.
