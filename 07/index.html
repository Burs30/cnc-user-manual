Security & Support

1

Open Security Settings

From your profile menu, tap Security Settings to manage passwords and two-factor authentication.

2

Change Your Password

Enter your current password, then set a new one. Must be 8+ characters with uppercase, number, and symbol.

3

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Tap Enable 2FA. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy, then enter the 6-digit setup code to confirm.

4

Sign Out Everywhere

If you suspect unauthorized access, use Sign Out to end all active sessions immediately.

5

Need Help?

Visit Contact from the main navigation for our location, hours, and phone number. Our team is happy to assist.

lightbulb Pro Tip:

Enable 2FA as soon as possible — it adds an essential layer of security to your account and only takes 30 seconds to set up.
