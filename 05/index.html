Managing Your Appointments

1

Open the Customer Portal

Click your Profile Icon → Customer Portal to see all your bookings in one place.

2

View Your Stats

At a glance see your Total Bookings, Upcoming, Completed, and Cancelled appointments.

3

Search & Filter

Use the Search bar to find appointments by service or barber. Filter by All, Upcoming, Completed, or Cancelled.

4

View Details & Actions

Tap View Details to see service, barber, date, time, price, and status. From here you can also Pay Deposit, Rate Visit, or Cancel.

5

Pay with QR Code

Tap Pay Deposit to open a PayMongo QR. Scan it with GCash or Maya. The system auto-detects your payment — no receipt upload needed.

6

Save Your Receipt

Tap Save Appointment inside any detail view to download your booking receipt as a PNG image.

lightbulb Pro Tip:

If you have an existing active booking, a deposit is required to secure additional slots. Keep an eye on the yellow alert banner!
